9 February 1843 Instruction Nauvoo, Illinois Willard Richards
William Clayton		 Teachings of Joseph Smith to guests at his home on a variety of subjects, found in the journals of Joseph Smith and William Clayton. Some passages from these accounts are found today in D&C Section 129.
2 April 1843 Instruction Macedonia, Illinois Willard Richards
William Clayton		 Teachings of Joseph Smith on various topics-primarily in response to questions, and clarification of others' remarks-found in the journals of Joseph Smith and William Clayton. Some passages from these accounts are found today in D&C Section 130.
